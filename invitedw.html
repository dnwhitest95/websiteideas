Super Smash Bro's Tournament

Event Details
  • Date: August 3rd 2016
  • Location: Brick City
  • Time: 5:00pm-10:00pm

The competition will consist of 4 round robins. The top eight players from the round robin will enter the tourament stage. The top player from the round robin will get to choose any of the 8 predetermied characters they please. The following seven players will then pick the remaining characters.
