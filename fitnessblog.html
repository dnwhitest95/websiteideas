We all know it's not possible to get in shape quickly. I'm sure some of you reading this have wondered this "How can I get a body that will leave me dripping in bitches by the summer time?" Well I can tell you this, working out will definitely lead to a lot of attention..mostly from dudes. In fact it's been pretty much all dudes, with the occasional grandmother.

Now that you got the basics down a can tell you a little bit more about myself. In highschool I was huge. After junior year I was 265 pounds and I was getting pretty close to being a size 44 waist. I'm not really sure why I decided to change up my life. I was fat but it was all I knew at time so I can't really remember how badly I wanted to change. I remember trying to alter my lifestyle a few times but never really putting in the necessary effort for it to make an impact. By January of 2013 I it was time to start thinking about colleges. I wasn't until then that I wanted to lose a few pounds so that I could actually get noticed by girls instead of just ice cream trucks. My parents were doing a Fast with the church as they do every January so I decided to join them. I was that Fast that changed my view, I decided that I needed to do better for myself, I knew that I could do better for myself. So well after the fast ended I continued to eat healthy. As I watched the weight drop off I began to want more for myself. By the time I was ready to go to school I had lost a decent amount of weight, I honestly can't remember how much. Once the semester began I realize the I should start goin even further in trip to reach a healthy weight by exercising. I began lifting weights freshman year of college and was extremely piss poor at it. Thank god I was already big and I didn't have to bulk up too much to gain muscle. Anyway as the years went on I lifted more and more and eat healthier and healthier, I'm to the point where now I own a fucking food scale whereas back in highschool the only food scale I used was the toilet bowl.
